🔍 What is this workshop?

You'll test AI image generators by writing prompts that reflect your identity and lived experience. We'll evaluate how well different systems represent diverse communities — and where they fall short.

Your perspective matters. AI systems often carry hidden biases that only become visible when people from underrepresented communities test them.

📋 How it works

  1. Shared Prompts — Rate 5 standard prompts everyone evaluates (baseline comparison)
  2. Free Exploration — Write your own prompts to probe specific biases
  3. Rate & Annotate — Score images on authenticity, diversity, and respectfulness
  4. View Results — See your personal evaluation dashboard
